Power BI dashboard project built on the AdventureWorks sales dataset to analyze revenue, profitability, orders, and customer behavior across time, products, and regions. The report highlights key KPIs including total revenue of $25M, total profit of $10.5M, 25.2K orders, and a 2.2% return rate, with monthly trends for revenue, orders, and profit. Interactive visuals break down performance by product category (Accessories, Bikes, Clothing), top 10 products with return percentages, and identify the most ordered and most returned product types (Tires and Tubes vs. Shorts). A customer analytics page tracks 10.5K customers, average revenue per customer of $874.6, weekly customer trends, and segmentation by income level and occupation (professional, skilled manual, management), including a ranked Top 100 customers table with revenue and order counts. Additional pages include a regional map view (Europe, North America, Pacific) and a product-level performance simulator that compares monthly orders and revenue vs. target, visualizes profit and adjusted profit trends, and allows dynamic price adjustment via a slider to study margin impact for a selected product such as “Mountain Tire Tube”. This project showcases skills in data modeling, DAX measures for KPIs and targets, advanced Power BI visuals, and storytelling for executive-ready sales and customer insights.
